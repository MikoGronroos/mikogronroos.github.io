Battle your friends in this addictive local multiplayer racing game.

Fly an ufo ship and claim more and more area to yourself to beat your enemies.

Collect special abilities like fill energy, speed boost and confuse enemy controls.

When the timer runs out the player who has claimed the most land wins.
